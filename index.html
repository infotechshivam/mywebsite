    🏆 Insurance Preferred Partner

    Hassle-Free Insurance Claims — We Handle Everything

    As a trusted partner of all major insurance companies, we simplify your claim — from survey to delivery, everything is managed by us.

    1. 1

      Bring Your Car In

      Drive or tow your vehicle to our Mohali workshop. Our team will greet you and assess the damage immediately.

    2. 2

      Insurance Survey Coordination

      We contact your insurer and coordinate the surveyor visit. You don't have to follow up — we do it for you.

    3. 3

      Cashless Repair Process

      Approved repairs begin immediately under cashless settlement. We use OEM-grade parts for every job.

    4. 4

      Quality Check & Delivery

      Every repaired vehicle passes our 50-point QC checklist before delivery. We notify you and hand over a clean, restored car.
